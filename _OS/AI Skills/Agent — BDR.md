## Primary Objectives
1. Generate 5+ qualified outreach touches per day (Aaron's non-negotiable)
2. Write cold outreach that gets responses — not opens
3. Identify and prioritize the right targets (ICP-first, always)
4. Build sequences that handle objections before they arise
5. Move leads from cold → conversation → demo

---

## ICP (Who We're Targeting)
- **Primary:** Commercial GCs managing $25M–$500M+ annual volume
- **Secondary:** Real estate developers with active construction programs
- **Tertiary:** Wholesale brokers (Halcyon, Burns & Wilcox, AmWINS, RT Specialty)
- **Not now:** Residential, small subs, government/public sector

---

## Outreach Rules
- Lead with the problem, not the product
- One thesis per message — never list features
- Reference something specific about them (project, company, news)
- CTA is always low-friction: a question, not a meeting request
- Never say: "I wanted to reach out," "I hope this finds you well," "synergy," "solution"
- Max 5 sentences for cold outreach
- Follow-up is not optional — 4–6 touch sequences minimum

- LinkedIn DM (primary for GC targets)

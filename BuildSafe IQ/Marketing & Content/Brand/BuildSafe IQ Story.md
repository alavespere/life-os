## The Origin

Construction is where money goes to die.

Not because builders are bad at their jobs — most of them are exceptional. It's because the information they need to make good decisions arrives too late, in the wrong format, or not at all.

A subcontractor starts struggling financially. Six months later, they're on your critical path and unable to perform. A project in your region hits a materials shortage. You're three weeks into your schedule before your supplier tells you. A GC you're evaluating has a track record of change order abuse and litigation. You find that out after you've signed.

The signals were always there. Nobody was reading them.

---

## The Insight

Aaron Lavespere spent years watching this pattern repeat across the construction industry. The data existed — in lien filings, payment histories, project records, contractor financials, market conditions, and dozens of other sources. What didn't exist was a system to aggregate it, interpret it, and deliver it as intelligence at the moment of decision.

That's the gap BuildSafe IQ was built to close.

---

## The Platform

BuildSafe IQ is an AI-powered construction risk intelligence platform. It monitors the leading indicators of project failure — subcontractor financial health, payment behavior, schedule performance, market conditions — and translates them into a clear risk picture that GCs, developers, and investors can actually use.

Not a dashboard full of data. A decision-support tool that tells you what matters and why.

---

## Who It's For

- **Commercial GCs** who need to qualify subcontractors and monitor project risk in real time
- **Subcontractors** who want to prove their performance and win better work
- **Developers** who are making capital commitments based on contractor execution risk
- **Investors** who are underwriting projects and need independent risk verification

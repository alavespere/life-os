## Color Palette

### Primary
| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Midnight Blue | `#1E2A38` | Headers, nav, key UI, executive materials |
| Steel Teal | `#4682A9` | CTAs, icons, graphs, highlights, interactive elements |

### Secondary
| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Safety Yellow | `#FFEB3B` | Risk alerts, warnings, critical notifications (use sparingly) |
| Slate Gray | `#7B8A99` | Body text, borders, inactive states, supporting elements |

### Accent
| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Smart Green | `#4CAF50` | Dashboards, success messages, positive metrics |
| Clean White | `#FFFFFF` | Background, negative space, readability |

---

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Montserrat
- **Usage:** Headlines, headers, logo text, key messaging
- **Personality:** Modern, geometric, authoritative
- **Weights:** Bold (700), SemiBold (600), Medium (500)

### Secondary Typeface: Open Sans (or Inter)
- **Usage:** Body text, captions, UI elements
- **Personality:** Clean, highly readable at small sizes

---

## Logo Direction
**Concept:** "Intelligent Construction Vision"

- Combines construction geometry with data/intelligence visualization
- Style: Clean, professional, scalable
- Must work at all sizes — from favicon to billboard
- Logo file: `buildsafe_logo.png` (in this folder)

**Personality the logo must convey:** Confident, intelligent, trustworthy, forward-thinking.

---

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — If it needs an explanation, simplify it
2. **Data as design** — Visualizations should be functional and beautiful
3. **Professional with an edge** — Sophisticated enough for C-suite, distinctive enough to stand out
4. **Construction-native** — Not generic SaaS; imagery and language grounded in the job site

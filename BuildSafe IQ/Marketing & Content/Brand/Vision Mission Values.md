## Vision
To be the most trusted source of construction risk intelligence in the industry — the platform that makes risk visible before it becomes a loss.

---

## Mission
BuildSafe IQ equips commercial GCs, subcontractors, developers, and investors with AI-powered risk data and analytics that turn uncertainty into competitive advantage.

---

## Values
- **Clarity over complexity** — We translate dense risk data into decisions people can act on
- **Data integrity** — We don't guess. We measure.
- **Builder-first** — We were built for the people who build things, not for insurance companies
- **Speed matters** — Construction moves fast. Our intelligence does too.
- **Accountability** — We stand behind our analysis

---

## About Us
BuildSafe IQ is an AI-powered construction risk intelligence platform. We aggregate project performance data, financial signals, subcontractor history, and market conditions to surface risk before it materializes — giving GCs, developers, and investors the information they need to protect margins, protect relationships, and make smarter bets.

---

## Our Story
Construction has always been a high-stakes industry — tight margins, complex projects, and risk that compounds fast. The traditional approach to managing that risk was reactive: wait for something to go wrong, then respond.

Aaron Lavespere founded BuildSafe IQ because he saw a different opportunity. The signals were always there — in project data, payment patterns, subcontractor behavior, and market conditions — they just weren't being read. With modern AI and the right data architecture, those signals could be turned into intelligence that actually helps builders make better decisions before a problem becomes a loss.

BuildSafe IQ is that intelligence layer.

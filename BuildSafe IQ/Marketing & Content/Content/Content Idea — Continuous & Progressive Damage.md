# Content Idea — Continuous & Progressive Damage Exclusions
_Added: 2026-04-08_
_Status: Idea — not drafted_

---

## Core Concept

Insurance denies claims not because damage didn't happen — but because it started **before the policy did**. Contractors don't know this until it's too late. BuildSafe IQ changes that.

## Hook Options

- "The insurance claim that was already denied before damage was ever found."
- "Your policy started in January. Your water intrusion started in December. You're not covered."
- "Continuous and progressive damage exclusions are one of the most misunderstood clauses in construction insurance."

## Key Points to Hit

1. What C&P damage is (water, mold, corrosion, settlement — no single event)
2. How insurance coverage is triggered by *occurrence*, not *discovery*
3. What C&P exclusions mean in plain language
4. Why early detection and reporting matters
5. How BuildSafe IQ surfaces these signals before coverage gaps form

## Audience
Commercial GCs, subcontractors, developers — anyone placing construction insurance.

## Format Options
- LinkedIn post (short, hook-driven)
- Long-form article (educational, SEO)
- Short video script (visual walkthrough of a C&P timeline)

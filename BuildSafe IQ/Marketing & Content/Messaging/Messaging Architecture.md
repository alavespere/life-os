## Master Message
**"BuildSafe IQ turns construction risk into a managed cost — before it becomes a loss."**

## Language Rules — Apply Everywhere

| Remove | Replace With |
|--------|-------------|
| "AI platform" | "tech-enabled risk advisory service" |
| "One platform integrating..." | "Delivered by certified risk professionals, powered by AI" |
| "Construction Risk Management Software" | "Tech-Enabled Construction Risk Services" |
| "We harness AI that..." | "We put expert intelligence in front of your team..." |
| "50+ data sources" (standalone) | "Seven validated risk signals, updated in real time" |
| "SaaS" or "software" or "dashboard" | Describe the service and the expert delivering it |

## Message Pillars

### Pillar 1: Predictive Intelligence
**"See Tomorrow's Problems Today"**

- We score subcontractor and project risk across seven validated signals before work begins
- The data exists — in permit records, financial statements, EMRs, claim histories, WIP schedules. Most GCs never look at it. We do.
- Pattern recognition: $2.4B in construction claims data across 145,000 claims — a dataset no competitor can purchase or replicate

Key lines:
- "While your competitors are reacting, you'll know what's coming."
- "Stop paying for claims that were predictable six months ago."
- "Before we signed a single client, we came to the table with $2.4 billion in construction claims data across 145,000 construction-specific claims."

---

### Pillar 2: Expert Delivery — Not a Dashboard
**"Intelligence Is Only Valuable When It Reaches the Right Hands"**

- Not a SaaS tool you configure yourself and hope someone reads
- Certified risk professionals deliver findings directly to the GC's risk team, CFO, and insurance team — in QBRs, advisory sessions, and pre-bid reviews
- The AI scores the risk. The expert turns the score into an action.

Key lines:
- "Software doesn't manage risk. Experts do. We give them better information than anyone else."
- "Delivered by certified risk professionals, powered by AI."

---

### Pillar 3: Financial Protection
**"Risk Is a Margin Problem"**

- The average $300M contractor loses $42M annually in preventable losses
- TCOR (Total Cost of Risk) visibility: Risk Financing, Direct Losses, Indirect Losses, Admin, Taxes
- Insurance placement backed by intelligence — not renewal-cycle guesswork

Key lines:
- "Every delayed project isn't just a schedule problem. It's a margin problem."
- "Every risk has a dollar value. We quantify it before it hits your P&L."

---

### Pillar 4: Defensible Moat
**"The Data No Competitor Can Replicate"**

- $2.4B in construction claims data across 145,000 claims — pre-loaded before first client
- Seven validated risk signals: financial health, safety record, claims history, license/bonding, project fit, geographic exposure, market conditions
- Grant Robbins co-founded Billy and was an original Procore validator — domain access no AI company can buy

Key lines:
- "Our dataset is the moat. You can't buy 145,000 construction claims. You have to build it."

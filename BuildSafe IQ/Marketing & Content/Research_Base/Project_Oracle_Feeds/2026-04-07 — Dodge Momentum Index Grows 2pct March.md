# Dodge Momentum Index Grows 2% in March

**Source:** Construction.com | **Date:** April 7, 2026

## Main Finding
DMI increased 1.8% in March to 250.5. Up 25.8% year-over-year. Growth is almost entirely driven by data center projects — strip that out and commercial planning is actually down 12.7%.

## Sector Data
| Segment | Monthly | YoY |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Commercial planning | +7.0% | +28.5% (+28.5% ex-data centers: -12.7%) |
| Institutional planning | -8.8% | +19.6% |

## Notable Projects in Pipeline
- Amazon Data Center Campus (NC): 17 buildings × $500M
- Microsoft Data Center DSM50 (IA): 10 buildings × $250M
- MCLJ Outpatient Pavilion (CA): $245M
- Orlando Health Viera Hospital (FL): $183M

## Risk Implications
- Data center concentration masks broad market weakness — GCs not in that sector face a softer pipeline
- Analyst note: "Elevated macroeconomic risks are likely beginning to feed into planning decisions"
- Institutional decline (-8.8%) worth watching — government/healthcare projects are a stabilizing segment for many regional GCs

## Relevance to BuildSafe IQ
Pipeline concentration risk is an underwriting signal. GCs over-indexed to data centers face different risk profiles than diversified contractors — this is exactly the kind of market-level context that should feed into project risk scoring.

# Insurance Analytics and AI Momentum — Majesco 2026 Strategic Priorities Report

**Source:** Carrier Management | **Date:** May 4, 2026 | **Author:** Denise Garth

## Main Argument
Data modernization is the new competitive battleground. Only 53% of insurers have access to all operational data — and those stuck on legacy on-premise systems are falling behind on AI/agentic intelligence. The gap between industry "Leaders," "Followers," and "Laggards" is widening.

## Key Data Points
- GenAI capabilities grew 19% YoY
- Agentic AI reached Planning/Piloting phase rapidly in 2026
- Underwriting AI use cases dropped 7 points — shifting from exploration to execution elsewhere
- Leaders: 47–60% adoption in high-impact areas; Laggards: minimal
- Top GenAI benefit: eliminating manual data input (4.2/5)
- GenAI outpaces Agentic AI by 0.2–0.5 points across the value chain

## Relevance to BuildSafe IQ
- Validates the market moment: carriers are actively prioritizing AI in underwriting infrastructure
- The "Leaders vs. Laggards" framing is pitch-deck ready — BuildSafe IQ sits at the intersection of data modernization and construction risk
- Underwriting use cases declining among large carriers = opportunity for niche risk intelligence platforms to fill the gap
- Agentic AI entering planning/piloting = tailwinds for platforms that surface structured risk data automatically

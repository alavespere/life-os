## Risk Implication for Construction

CPI at +3.8% YoY means inflation is re-accelerating. For construction:

- **Materials cost volatility** continues — fixed-price contracts without escalation clauses are risk transfer to GCs
- **Labor cost inflation** compounds WC premiums (WC premiums are wage-indexed)
- **Subcontractor margin compression** — firms with 2–4% net margin cannot absorb a 3.8% cost environment without change orders
- **Framing specifically exposed**: lumber price volatility + general inflation + thin margins = elevated default risk in fixed-price framing contracts

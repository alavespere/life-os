### [Insurance] Travelers Report: Aging Workforce Driving Longer WC Recovery Times
Travelers (2026-05-11): Aging workforce + new employees driving complexity in injury claims. Recovery times growing longer even as workplace injury rates decline.

**Risk Implication for Construction:** Two-part exposure:
1. Aging field workers = more severe injuries when they occur (longer recovery, higher WC costs)
2. New employees (high turnover trades) = higher frequency at entry-level tasks

This reinforces the trade-calibrated risk scoring framework. A framing sub with a young/new crew is a different risk profile than one with experienced workers — even with the same TRIR.

**Content Seed:** "The WC Problem Nobody Talks About: Your Workers Are Getting Older and Your Claims Are Getting Worse"

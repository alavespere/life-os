### ✅ Construction / Contractor GL Lines Softening
GL for contractors: flat renewals, capacity returning, E&S remains the preferred channel for complex construction risks. Market is competitive but selective — submission quality and data depth are the differentiator.

**Risk Implication:** This is our operating environment. Better submissions = better pricing. BuildSafe IQ's data layer adds direct value at placement.

---

### ✅ SAM Coverage — Underserved Gap
Demand for Sexual Abuse & Molestation coverage spiking for trade contractors and GCs working at schools, healthcare facilities, and municipalities. New capacity entering but still largely E&S placed.

**Action:** Specific line to pursue through Burns & Wilcox. Flag for brokerage pipeline.

### ✅ Environmental / CPL — Soft Market, Good Placement Opportunity
Contractors' Pollution Liability competitively priced with new entrants. Good placement window for GC clients. PFAS/ethylene oxide creating exclusion complexity at the excess level — risk intelligence layer adds value flagging exposures early.

---

### ⚠️ A&E Under Pressure
Architects & Engineers facing inflation on materials, remediation, and labor costs. Supply chain compounding loss costs. Tighter terms expected for design-build or A&E-adjacent clients.

---

### ⚠️ Casualty Social Inflation — Dominant Theme
Every section of the B&W report flags litigation funding, rising severity, and social inflation. Plays directly into risk scoring value prop — carriers want better-quality submissions from better-quality risks.

## ✅ Construction / Contractor Lines Softening
GL for contractors seeing flat renewals, capacity returning, E&S remains preferred channel for complex construction risks. Market is competitive but selective — submission quality and data depth are the differentiator. This is BSIQ's operating environment and core value prop.

## ✅ SAM Coverage — Underserved Gap
Demand for Sexual Abuse & Molestation coverage spiking for trade and GCs working at schools, healthcare facilities, municipalities. New capacity entering but still largely E&S placed. Specific line to pursue through Burns & Wilcox.

## ✅ Environmental (CPL) — Soft, Good Placement Opportunity
Contractors' Pollution Liability competitively priced with new entrants. Good placement opportunity for GC clients. PFAS/ethylene oxide still creating exclusion complexity at the excess level — BSIQ risk intelligence layer adds value flagging exposures early.

## ⚠️ A&E Under Pressure
Architects & Engineers facing inflation on materials, remediation, and labor costs. Supply chain issues compounding loss costs. Expect tighter terms for design-build or A&E-adjacent clients.

## ⚠️ Casualty Social Inflation — Dominant Theme
Every section of the report flags litigation funding, rising severity, social inflation. Plays directly into BSIQ risk scoring value prop — carriers want better-quality submissions from better-quality risks. We help brokers build that story.

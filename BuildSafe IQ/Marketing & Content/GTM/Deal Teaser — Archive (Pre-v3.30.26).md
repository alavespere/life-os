## Overview (Archive)

**Company:** BuildSafe IQ
**Ask (old):** $3.5M seed
**Pre-money (old):** $17.5M

**Problem:** Construction projects fail at staggering rates. Cost overruns, schedule delays, and subcontractor failures destroy margins — and the industry has no early warning system.

**Solution:** BuildSafe IQ is an AI-powered risk intelligence platform that monitors the signals of project failure in real time — giving GCs, developers, and investors a live view of risk before it becomes a loss.

**Model:** SaaS subscriptions. Annual contracts. Enterprise pricing.

**Traction:** Early beta engagement with commercial GC partners. Data partnerships in development.

**Team:** Aaron Lavespere (Founder/CEO), Ev Jordan (VP Technology), advisors with 30+ years construction industry experience.

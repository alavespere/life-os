## Industry Challenge Section

**Headline:** "Why Construction Risk Management Is Broken"

**Challenge 1: Reactive vs. Predictive**
> The industry responds to problems after they emerge. This reactive approach costs the average $300M contractor $42M annually in preventable losses.

**Challenge 2: Siloed vs. Integrated**
> Risk data lives in disconnected systems across organizations, preventing the holistic view needed to identify patterns between risk factors.

**Challenge 3: Anecdotal vs. Analytical**
> Decision-making relies on gut feeling rather than systematic analysis of project data — leading to the same mistakes, project after project.

**Challenge 4: Generic vs. Specific**
> Standard risk management treats all projects the same, ignoring the unique risk profile of different project types, locations, and team compositions.

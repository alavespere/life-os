# Stealth Mode Website Strategy
_BuildSafe IQ Inc. — Last updated: 2026-04-06_
_Status: Active — pre-funding phase_

---

## Strategic Objective
Establish professional market presence that supports fundraising and early BDR activity — without revealing proprietary technology, algorithms, or methodologies.

---

## The Core Approach: Problem-Focused Authority
Demonstrate deep expertise in **identifying and articulating construction risk problems** — without revealing the proprietary methods for solving them.

Show you understand the pain better than anyone else. Let the solution remain implicit.

---

## What to Show vs. What to Protect

| Show | Protect |
|------|---------|
| Deep understanding of industry problems | Specific algorithms and scoring methodology |
| Team credentials and experience | Data architecture and integration approach |
| High-level platform concept | Pricing model and unit economics |
| Market size and opportunity | Client names and deal structure |
| Industry research and insights | Proprietary data sources |

---

## Website Structure

### Homepage
- **Hero:** Bold problem statement + "Request Executive Briefing" CTA
- **Problem Framework:** Construction risk landscape visualization
- **Why Now:** Market urgency, technology inflection point
- **Team:** Credentials-forward — industry experience and data science backgrounds
- **CTA:** "Request Executive Briefing" → BDR team

### Industry Insights (Blog)
- Narrative-driven content following Johnny Harris framework
- Problem analysis — deep dives on industry challenges
- Market research — data-backed industry statistics
- Case studies — anonymized problem scenarios (not solution specifics)
- Establishes authority without revealing IP

### About
- Vision and mission: industry transformation narrative
- Team: expertise-forward, not title-forward
- Strategic approach: high-level methodology, no technical details

### Contact
- Primary CTA: "Request Executive Briefing"
- For investors: subtle pathway for qualified interest

---

## IP Protection Rules
1. No technical documentation or methodology details on site
2. No specific algorithm descriptions
3. No client names or deal specifics
4. No pricing or financial projections
5. No platform screenshots showing proprietary features

---

## Content Strategy (Stealth Phase)
Focus content on the problem, not the solution:
- "Why 70% of construction risk is invisible until it's too late"
- "The data that predicts project failure — and where to find it"
- "What construction claims data actually tells us about project health"

Each piece establishes authority on the problem space. The solution is implied, not revealed.

---

## Success Metrics (Stealth Phase)
- Investor interest and inbound inquiries
- BDR-generated meeting requests from target ICP
- Brand awareness in target GC market (NH / New England first)
- Zero IP leakage

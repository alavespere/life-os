## Core Narrative
_(Extracted from source — align with current pitch framing before use)_

Construction is a $2T+ industry in the US alone. It runs on thin margins, complex projects, and a chronic inability to see risk coming. The insurance and risk management systems supporting construction have not kept pace with the data and AI capabilities now available.

BuildSafe IQ is the intelligence layer the industry is missing.

We aggregate the signals of project performance, subcontractor health, financial behavior, and market conditions — and deliver them as actionable risk intelligence to the GCs, developers, and investors making multi-million dollar decisions every day.

**The market is large. The problem is acute. The timing is right.**

# Go-to-Market Strategy & Partnership Framework
_BuildSafe IQ Inc. — Last updated: 2026-04-06_

---

## Strategic Objective
Establish market dominance in construction risk intelligence within 18 months.
Target: Become the largest construction data aggregator in the US and the #1 risk management + insurance broker in the construction industry.

---

## Target Market (Primary)
**Commercial GCs doing $100M–$500M in annual construction volume**

Why this segment:
- Large enough to have complex, recurring risk exposure
- Small enough that enterprise-level risk intelligence has been inaccessible
- High willingness to pay for margin protection
- Decision-maker accessible (CEO, CFO, COO — not procurement committee)

---

## GTM Phases

### Phase 1: Authority Building (Months 1–6)
**Objective:** Establish BuildSafe IQ as the voice that challenges industry assumptions

Key moves:
- Weekly content output (LinkedIn + blog) using Johnny Harris narrative framework
- Industry association presence: ABC, AGC, CFMA
- Trade media relationships: ENR, Construction Dive, Risk & Insurance
- BDR-driven outreach — content opens the door, BDR books the meeting

Target topics for authority building:
- "Why your insurance broker is accidentally sabotaging your profit margin"
- "The $2 trillion industry that can't predict tomorrow's weather"
- "Construction's dirty secret: everyone knows projects will fail"

---

### Phase 2: Market Penetration (Months 7–12)
**Objective:** Convert authority into pipeline

Key moves:
- Geographic focus: Start in NH/New England, expand to target metros
- BDR-enabled sales model: content → inbound interest → BDR qualifies → Aaron closes
- Wholesale broker partnerships (Halcyon, Burns & Wilcox, AmWINS, RT Specialty) as referral channel
- Case study production from early clients

---

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 13–18)
**Objective:** Systematize what works, expand geography

Key moves:
- Hire first dedicated sales rep (post-funding)
- Formalize partnership agreements with wholesale brokers
- Launch risk scoring product as standalone offering
- Expand to 3–5 target metros

---

## Distribution Channels

### Primary: Direct (BDR + Aaron)
- Content-led inbound → BDR qualification → Aaron close
- Average deal: Risk management retainer + insurance placement

### Secondary: Wholesale Broker Referrals
- Partners: Halcyon, Burns & Wilcox, AmWINS, RT Specialty
- Model: Brokers refer clients with complex construction risk for placement + intelligence layer

### Tertiary: Industry Associations
- ABC, AGC, CFMA membership → speaking → relationships → referrals

## What BuildSafe IQ Does
We monitor the leading indicators of project failure and give you a clear risk picture before problems surface:

- **Subcontractor financial health** — payment behavior, bonding capacity, credit signals
- **Project performance tracking** — schedule risk, milestone slippage, RFI patterns
- **Market signals** — labor, materials, regional risk factors
- **Live alerts** — when something changes, you know first

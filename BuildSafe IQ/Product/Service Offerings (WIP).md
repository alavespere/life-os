## Core Offerings

### 1. Risk Intelligence Platform (SaaS)
Subscription access to the BuildSafe IQ platform:
- Real-time project risk monitoring
- Subcontractor financial health tracking
- Market and labor signal feeds
- Risk scoring and alerts
- Reporting and export tools

**Audience:** Commercial GCs, developers, investors
**Pricing:** Annual subscription (tiered by firm size and project volume)

---

### 2. Live Risk Management Service
Dedicated risk management support layered on top of the platform:
- Dedicated Risk Manager assigned to client account
- Monthly risk review calls
- Pre-bid subcontractor vetting reports
- Project kickoff risk assessments
- Incident response support

**Audience:** GCs with complex project mix or high subcontractor exposure
**Pricing:** Service fee in addition to platform subscription

---

### 3. Pre-Bid Subcontractor Intelligence Reports
One-time or recurring reports on specific subcontractors:
- Financial health summary
- Payment behavior history
- Bonding capacity
- Project performance record
- Red flags and risk indicators

**Audience:** GCs evaluating new subs or large subcontract awards
**Pricing:** Per-report or subscription bundle

---

### 4. Investor / Lender Risk Verification (Future)
Independent risk assessment for development projects:
- GC and subcontractor qualification
- Project timeline and budget risk scoring
- Construction risk report for lender underwriting

**Audience:** Real estate developers, construction lenders, PE/family offices
**Status:** Future offering — not in current model

---

## Packaging Approach
Current model bundles platform + service into tiered annual contracts (Tiers 1–5, $50K–$110K/year). Standalone report pricing TBD.

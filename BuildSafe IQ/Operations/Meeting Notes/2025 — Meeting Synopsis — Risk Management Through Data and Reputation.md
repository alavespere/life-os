## Summary
Meeting discussion centered on how data-driven risk assessment and reputation signals can be used to improve construction risk management outcomes.

- Reputation signals (payment history, project performance, litigation, lien filings) are leading indicators — not lagging
- The gap between available data and how it's actually used in underwriting and project selection is massive
- AI can close that gap at scale

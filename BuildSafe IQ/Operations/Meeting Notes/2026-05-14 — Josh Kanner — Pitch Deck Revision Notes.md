### Slide 7 — Data / Signal Architecture (Full Rebuild)
**Current:** Generic "50+ data sources → AI engine → Unified Risk Index." No specificity.
**Problem:** Josh was disappointed there was no data science or AI depth. This is what he built at Newmetrix. He knows the difference between a marketing claim and a technical position.

**Rename to: "The BuildSafe Risk Score — Signal Architecture"**

**Seven Primary Risk Signals:**

| Signal | What It Measures | Data Source |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| Financial Health Trajectory | D&B Paydex direction, credit rating changes, days-payable trends. Direction matters more than snapshot. | D&B, credit bureaus |
| Revenue-to-Capacity Ratio | Active backlog vs. bonding capacity vs. prior-year revenue. Flags overextension before default. | Bonding limits, permit data, bid submissions |
| Billing-to-Completion Mismatch | Billed % vs. actual work-in-place. The leading indicator in the $19M case study — 72% billed, 55% complete. | GC project data (via service relationship) |
| Payment Dispute & Lien History | Mechanics liens, payment bond claims, court-filed disputes. Fully public record. | County court records, lien databases |
| OSHA Violation Frequency & Severity | Recordable incident rate vs. NAICS industry average. Trend direction, not just existence. Willful vs. other-than-serious. | OSHA public database |
| License & Compliance Currency | Contractor license status, COI currency, carrier AM Best rating. Lapses often precede default. | State licensing boards, COI verification |
| Project Concentration Risk | % of revenue tied to one GC, owner, or project type. Measures dependency exposure. | Project data, bid history |

**Score Output:**
- 0–100 composite index, weighted by project type and contract size
- Green (70–100): Standard monitoring
- Yellow (40–69): Elevated watch — flag in next QBR
- Red (0–39): Immediate advisory — hold, additional bonding, or contract restructure

**The Feedback Loop — This Is the Moat:**
> Every QBR produces an outcome. Right or wrong, the prediction is logged. Over time, BuildSafe IQ builds a proprietary dataset of construction risk outcomes tied to leading indicator signals. No carrier, no broker, no platform has this dataset. This is what makes the model defensible at scale — the same way Newmetrix built its moat through outcome data on jobsite safety.

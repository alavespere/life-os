## Suggested Talking Points

### Open with his world, not ours
- "Vitaliy mentioned you've been building in the NH commercial real estate market for a while — how are you thinking about risk management across your portfolio right now?"
- Let him describe his problem before you describe your solution.

### Connect to his operational reality
- He owns a hotel and coworking space — both involve contractors, subcontractors, liability exposure, insurance compliance.
- The COI verification problem and subcontractor risk are not abstract concepts for him. He's lived them.

### Frame BuildSafe IQ at his level
- "We're building the intelligence layer that sits between the construction project and the insurance market — so that GCs, developers, and brokers can make better risk decisions with data instead of intuition."
- Mention: AI-powered prequalification, risk scoring, and COI analysis. Not "software for contractors" — **risk intelligence for anyone with construction exposure.**

### LaunchNH fit
- $40M fund, AI/SaaS mandate, early-stage. BuildSafe IQ is pre-seed, fits the thesis.
- If there's investor interest: "We're in conversations with a few pre-seed funds now — would LaunchNH be the right fit to talk about participating?"

### Close with a clear next step
- Either: follow-up call with more detail, or sharing the deck directly.

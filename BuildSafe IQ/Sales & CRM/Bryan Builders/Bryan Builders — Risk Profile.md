## Key Risk Flags

### Client Concentration Risk
- **Single client dependency:** 100% revenue from Amazon
- Any change in Amazon's vendor strategy or project pipeline = direct revenue impact
- No diversification buffer

### Subcontractor Risk
- Heavy reliance on a small pool of traveling subcontractors
- Subcontractor shortage limiting scalability and timelines
- Subcontractors not fully utilizing the risk management system — compliance gap

### Group Captive Concerns
- Heterogeneous group — members without robust risk controls raise collective exposure
- Insurance rates above industry averages due to group heterogeneity
- Lack of stringent risk processes across all captive members

### Workforce Risk
- Preference for hiring and training green employees over experienced personnel
- Cultural integration vs. expertise tradeoff
- Inexperienced workforce = elevated operational and safety risk

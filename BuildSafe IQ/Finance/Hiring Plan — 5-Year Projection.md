## Hiring Principles
1. **Risk-first:** Every hire in Years 1–2 must strengthen the core risk intelligence product
2. **Outsource non-core:** Dev, marketing, and legal outsourced until revenue justifies in-house
3. **Earn the brokerage:** Brokerage hires contingent on RM revenue hitting Year 2 targets
4. **Field before HQ:** Field Risk Specialists hired before HQ expansion

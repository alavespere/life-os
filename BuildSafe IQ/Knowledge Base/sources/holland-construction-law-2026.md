## Master Theme

> Construction risk is NOT random — it is predictable from contract language, documentation quality, insurance structure, and project delivery method. Courts consistently reward parties who document thoroughly, give timely notice, define responsibilities in writing, and maintain contemporaneous records.

This is BSIQ's operating thesis operationalized at the legal level.

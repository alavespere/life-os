# Burns & Wilcox — P&C Q2 2026 Market Intel

**Source:** Burns & Wilcox Q2 2026 P&C Market Outlook
**Processed:** 2026-05-07
**Relevance:** B&W is a primary target wholesale broker distribution channel for BSIQ brokerage JV

---

## Five Key Signals

### 1. AI/API Platforms for Binding Authority — Explicitly Validated
B&W validates AI and API-enabled platforms for binding authority workflows. **Direct alignment with BSIQ's pitch.** This is the clearest wholesale broker validation of BSIQ's tech-enabled model as of Q2 2026. Cite in pitch deck and broker conversations.

### 2. Construction GL — Softening Market, Submission Quality Differentiates
GL market softening slightly but submission quality remains the differentiator for pricing. GCs who come in with organized, documented subcontractor risk profiles get better terms. This is BSIQ's value prop at the broker interface.

### 3. SAM Coverage — Spike in Demand for GCs at Schools/Healthcare/Municipalities
Sexual Abuse and Molestation (SAM) coverage demand increasing for GCs working on school, healthcare, and municipal projects. These owners are requiring SAM endorsements in subcontracts. Potential new specialty line for the brokerage JV.

### 4. CPL Market (Environmental) — Soft, New Entrants
Contractors Pollution Liability market is soft with new entrants. Good placement opportunity for site prep, HVAC, painting, and below-grade work subcontractors.

### 5. Social Inflation — Dominant Theme Across All Lines
Social inflation cited across all construction lines as the primary claims driver. See [[social-inflation]] for full analysis.

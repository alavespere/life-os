# Newmetrix

AI-powered construction risk intelligence platform. Acquired by Oracle Construction & Engineering in 2022. Now integrated into Oracle's construction management suite.

**Josh Kanner is Senior Director of Product & Strategy at Oracle Construction & Engineering — he co-founded Newmetrix.** He is BSIQ's most valuable domain expert connection.

---

## What Newmetrix Did (Pre-Acquisition)

- Safety and risk intelligence for construction projects
- AI analysis of project data to predict safety incidents and cost/schedule overruns
- Served GCs and large construction programs
- Founded by Josh Kanner and team; sold to Oracle 2022

Josh also previously co-founded Vela Systems (construction field management, sold to Autodesk 2012) — serial exit in construction tech. His feedback on the BSIQ pitch carries extreme credibility.

---

## Post-Acquisition Status

Integrated into Oracle Construction & Engineering (Primavera). Oracle's focus is large enterprise construction management — project controls, scheduling (Primavera P6), and now AI-augmented risk for enterprise customers.

Oracle is listed as a potential M&A acquirer in BSIQ's pitch deck. Josh Kanner is aware of this context and continued the conversation.

---

## Josh Kanner's Feedback on BSIQ (May 2026)

Four critiques of the original pitch:
1. TAM sourcing — needs to be more credible
2. Boiling the ocean — scope too broad; must focus
3. Platform vs. service — "sounds like a copied platform"; should be tech-enabled service
4. No AI/data science depth — investors expect to see methodology

All four incorporated into pitch deck v5. Josh agreed to continue the conversation after revision. **Target: make him an advisor, not an investor.** Advisor ask reserved for follow-up session (not email).

---

## Strategic Significance

Having Josh Kanner as an advisor would give BSIQ:
- Domain credibility from the person who built and sold the closest prior comp
- Oracle relationship context (potential M&A path awareness)
- Network access in construction tech VC and enterprise buyer space

# Zero RFI

AI-enabled owner's representative firm. Raised $13.8M seed from General Catalyst in March 2026. Serves building **owners and developers** — not GCs.

---

## What Zero RFI Does

An "owner's rep firm" where the rep is augmented by AI. They manage construction projects on behalf of building owners — overseeing GC performance, tracking project health, managing documentation, and advising owners on risk. The AI layer makes their advisors faster and more consistent.

---

## Why This Is a Direct Validation of BSIQ's Thesis

Zero RFI's model: AI is the engine → human professionals deliver the service → service fees generate revenue. This is exactly the model BSIQ is pivoting to.

General Catalyst's $13.8M seed investment in Zero RFI (March 2026) validates:
1. The tech-enabled advisory service model is fundable
2. Institutional investors believe AI + human expert > pure SaaS in construction
3. The market is large enough for multiple segment-specific entrants

**The key difference:** Zero RFI serves the **building owner**. BSIQ serves the **GC**. Different buyer, different problem, no direct competition.

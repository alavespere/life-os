# Procore Technologies

The dominant construction management platform. ~$10B+ market cap. Serves project managers, GCs, and owners with document management, project coordination, RFI tracking, and financial management tools.

---

## The Datagrid Acquisition (January 2026)

Procore acquired Datagrid to add AI-powered workflow automation for:
- Submittal automation
- RFI drafting
- Data connectivity across project documents

**Critical distinction for BSIQ:** Datagrid's AI is workflow automation — it helps project managers process paperwork faster. It does NOT score subcontractor risk, predict performance, or assess financial health. Their buyer is the **project manager**. BSIQ's buyer is the **risk manager, CFO, and insurance team**.

Same company, completely different stakeholder. Different problem. Different budget.

---

## Why Procore Acquiring Datagrid Is a Positive Signal for BSIQ

1. Validates AI in construction tech (broadly)
2. Confirms institutional capital is flowing into the space
3. Datagrid's workflow focus leaves risk intelligence unaddressed within Procore's platform
4. Procore's existing customer base (GCs) is BSIQ's primary target — Procore won't compete for risk budget; BSIQ can integrate or complement

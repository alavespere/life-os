# Trade Coverage Matrix

Reference for coverage requirements by trade vertical. Source: BuildSafe IQ Trade Intelligence research (2026-05-12).

---

## Standard Required Coverages (All Trades)

Every subcontractor regardless of trade:

| Coverage | Minimum Limits | Critical Endorsements |
|---|---|---|
| CGL | $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate | CG 20 10 + CG 20 37; Primary & Non-Contributory; Waiver of Subrogation |
| Workers' Compensation | Statutory per state | Waiver of Subrogation |
| Employers' Liability | $1M / $1M / $1M | — |
| Commercial Auto | $1M CSL | AI endorsement |
| Umbrella/Excess | $5M–$25M (project-dependent) | Follow-form over all underlying |

## Specialty Coverage by Trade

| Coverage | When Required | Trades |
|---|---|---|
| **CPL (Contractors Pollution Liability)** | Refrigerants, chemicals, near waterways, excavation, lead/solvent paint | HVAC (req'd), Site Prep (req'd), Painting (lead/solvent), Electrical (haz locations), Concrete (waterways) |
| **Professional Liability (E&O)** | Design-build or design-assist scope | Electrical, HVAC/Mechanical, Concrete (if design), Site Prep (civil design) |
| **Riggers Liability** | Crane operations, heavy lifts | Steel Erection (req'd), Concrete (large crane pours), HVAC (large equipment lifts) |
| **Hot Work Endorsement** | Torch work, welding, soldering | HVAC/Plumbing (req'd), Roofing (torch-applied, req'd), Steel/Welding (req'd) |
| **Installation Floater** | Equipment/materials in transit or during installation | Steel/Precast (req'd), Glass & Glazing |
| **Silica Liability** | Crystalline silica under OSHA 1926.1153 | Masonry (req'd), Concrete (rec'd), Drywall/Insulation (cutting) |
| **Extended Completed Ops (5+ yr)** | Long-tail exposure | Roofing (req'd), Fire Sprinkler (req'd), Steel, HVAC, Electrical |
| **Arc Flash** | Electrical work — panels, switchgear | Electrical (rec'd, increasingly required) |

## GL Risk Tier by Trade

| GL Risk Tier | Trades | GL Minimums |
|---|---|---|
| **Extreme** | Roofing, Steel Erection | $2M/$4M + $5–10M umbrella |
| **Very High** | Fire Sprinkler, HVAC/Plumbing | $2M/$4M recommended |
| **High** | Concrete, Masonry, Electrical | $1M/$2M min; $2M/$4M preferred |
| **Moderate** | Drywall, Painting, Site Prep, Flooring | $1M/$2M standard |

## Insurance Red Flags (Any Trade)

| Red Flag | Risk |
|---|---|
| Carrier below AM Best A- / VII | No guarantee of claim payment |
| Claims-made policy for long-tail trade | Sunset risk when policy lapses |
| Shared aggregate across multiple projects | One large claim on another project can exhaust aggregate |
| Completed ops sunset at 2 years | Insufficient for roofing, fire sprinkler, steel |
| COI shows coverage, no endorsements provided | Certificate ≠ policy |
| Non-admitted carrier for Extreme-tier trades | E&S acceptable only if A- rated; verify exclusions |
